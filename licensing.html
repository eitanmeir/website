Licensing

Last updated: November 24, 2025

1. Overview

Eitan & Meir GmbH ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") offers various licensing options for our educational products, including Circubes®, educational content, curriculum materials, and related intellectual property. This page outlines the licensing terms and options available for different types of users.

2. Product Licensing

2.1 Personal Use License

When you purchase Circubes® or other physical products for personal use, you receive a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the products for educational purposes within your household.

  • Valid for personal, non-commercial use only
  • May be used for home education and learning activities
  • Cannot be resold, rented, or sublicensed

2.2 Classroom License

Teachers and individual classrooms may use our products under a Classroom License:

  • Valid for use within a single classroom or learning group
  • Covers up to 30 students per classroom
  • Includes access to digital curriculum materials and teacher resources
  • Products remain the property of the purchasing institution

2.3 School/Institution License

Schools, universities, and educational institutions may purchase an Institution License:

  • Valid for use across multiple classrooms within a single institution
  • Number of users specified in purchase agreement
  • Includes educator training and professional development resources
  • Access to priority support and curriculum updates
  • Optional maintenance and replacement program

2.4 District/Enterprise License

School districts, educational organizations, and large-scale deployments may obtain a District License:

  • Valid for use across multiple institutions or locations
  • Customizable to organizational needs
  • Includes train-the-trainer programs and implementation support
  • Dedicated account management
  • Volume pricing and flexible payment terms

3. Educational Content Licensing

3.1 Curriculum Materials

Our curriculum materials, lesson plans, activity guides, and educational content are licensed as follows:

  • Included Content: Materials provided with product purchases may be used in accordance with the applicable product license
  • Attribution: When sharing or presenting our materials, proper attribution to Eitan & Meir GmbH is required
  • Modification: Educators may adapt materials for their specific teaching needs but may not distribute modified versions
  • Digital Sharing: Sharing materials with students within your licensed scope is permitted; public distribution is not

3.2 Commercial Content Use

Organizations wishing to use our content for commercial purposes (e.g., training programs, publications, workshops) must obtain a separate Commercial Content License. Contact us at licensing@eitan-meir.de.

4. Software and Digital Platform Licensing

Any software, mobile apps, or digital platforms provided by Eitan & Meir are licensed, not sold:

Select educational materials may be released under open licenses (e.g., Creative Commons). These materials will be clearly marked with the applicable license terms. Unless explicitly stated, all content is proprietary and subject to the licensing terms outlined on this page.

7. License Duration and Renewal

  • Product Licenses: Valid for the lifetime of the physical product under normal use
  • Subscription Services: Valid for the duration of the active subscription period

  • Content Access: Digital content access may require active license or subscription status

We reserve the right to verify license compliance. Organizations using our products may be required to:
