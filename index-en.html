About Us

Eitan & Meir GmbH is a Berlin-based educational technology company specializing in tools and platforms that make learning computer science, STEM, and artificial intelligence accessible and engaging for learners of all ages. We design modular systems that combine hands-on learning with digital innovation to empower the next generation of thinkers, creators, and problem solvers.

Our Focus Areas

  • Interactive Modular Learning Kits (e.g., Circubes)
  • Computer Science & Programming Education
  • AI and Machine Learning Concepts for Beginners
  • STEM-focused Hardware & Software Development
  • Educational Content & Curriculum Design
  • Collaborative Partnerships with Schools & Universities

Our Mission

We aim to close the gap between technology and education by creating intuitive, sustainable, and scalable solutions for learners, educators, and institutions. Through our products, we encourage curiosity, experimentation, and long-term learning in a rapidly evolving digital world.
