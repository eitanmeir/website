What is SynapTile™

SynapTile transforms abstract neural network concepts into physical tiles that students can touch, connect, and manipulate, making complex AI concepts accessible to beginners while maintaining depth for advanced exploration.

Physical Perceptron

Build a working perceptron with physical components. Each tile represents a neuron or connection in the network, bringing AI to life.

Visualize Neural Networks

Watch neural network operations happen in real-time as you manipulate tiles and see how data flows through the network.

Understand AI Fundamentals

Grasp weights, biases, and activation functions through hands-on experimentation. Perfect for introducing AI/ML in classrooms.

Beginner to Advanced

Accessible to beginners learning AI basics while offering depth for advanced exploration of machine learning principles.

Classroom Ready

Designed for group learning and collaborative problem-solving. Ideal for schools, universities, and workshops.

Make AI Tangible

Transform abstract concepts into something students can touch, connect, and manipulate—bridging theory and practice.

What is Balance

Balance is an educational mobile game that teaches mathematical thinking through interactive number harmony challenges. Players balance left and right values by strategically selecting numbers, developing problem-solving skills, numerical intuition, and mathematical reasoning in a fun and engaging way.

Number Harmony

Master the art of balancing numbers. Players learn to calculate differences and strategically select numbers to achieve perfect balance between left and right sides.

Strategic Thinking

Develop critical thinking skills by planning moves ahead. Each decision affects the balance, teaching players to think strategically about number selection and placement.

Real-Time Feedback

See the difference between left and right values instantly. Visual feedback helps players understand mathematical relationships and learn from each move.

Score & Progress

Track your progress with scores and move counts. Challenge yourself to achieve higher scores with fewer moves, encouraging efficient problem-solving.

Progressive Levels

Carefully designed levels that increase in complexity. Each level builds on previous skills, ensuring players are always challenged but never overwhelmed.

Perfect for Ages 9+

Designed specifically for children and learners ages 9 and up. No prior math knowledge required—accessible to beginners while building strong numerical foundations.

What is JuniorCoder

JuniorCoder is an educational mobile game designed to teach children fundamental programming concepts through interactive, visual programming. Children program a robot to reach a star by creating sequences of movement commands, learning computational thinking skills in a fun and engaging way.

Visual Programming

Complex programming concepts made simple through visual representation. Children see their programs as step-by-step sequences, making abstract ideas tangible.

Sequential Thinking

Learn that programs execute commands in order, one after another. Children understand that sequence matters and each command has a specific effect.

Step Planning

Break down problems into smaller steps. Children learn to think ahead, plan their solution, and review their program before executing.

Debugging Skills

Learn that programs don't always work the first time. Children develop problem-solving skills by identifying errors and fixing them through trial and error.

Progressive Levels

Carefully designed levels that build skills gradually. Each level introduces new concepts while building on previous knowledge, ensuring children are always challenged but never overwhelmed.

Perfect for Ages 4+

Designed specifically for children ages 4 and up. No prior programming knowledge required—accessible to complete beginners while teaching real computational thinking skills.
